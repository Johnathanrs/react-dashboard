About Evolute

Evolute makes the datacenter simpler by providing a software ecosystem optimized for IT administrators.

By integrating server, storage and network resources into a turnkey hyper-converged platform, our datacenter control plane makes it easy to be production ready from day one.

The Evolute DCP is built around a distributed systems kernel, container operating systems, and service discovery engines in production at companies like Netflix, Yelp and Twitter.
